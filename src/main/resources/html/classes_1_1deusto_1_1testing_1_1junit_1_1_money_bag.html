Detailed Description

A MoneyBag defers exchange rate conversions. For example adding 12 Swiss Francs to 14 US Dollars is represented as a bag containing the two Money objects associated to 12 CHF and 14 USD. Adding another 10 Swiss francs gives a MoneyBag with 22 CHF and 14 USD. Due to the deferred exchange rate conversion we can later value a MoneyBag with different exchange rates.

A MoneyBag is represented as a list of Money objects and provides different constructors to create a MoneyBag.
